About Cloud Kenya Host

Our Story

Founded in Nairobi in 2020, Cloud Kenya Host was born from a simple idea: Kenyan businesses deserve world-class hosting with local support. We saw a gap in the market for reliable, fast, and secure hosting that understands the unique needs of African businesses.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of customers across Kenya and East Africa, providing enterprise-grade infrastructure at affordable prices. Our team of experts works 24/7 to ensure your websites are always online, fast, and secure.
